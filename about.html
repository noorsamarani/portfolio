About

I’m Noor Samarani, a Lebanese UX/UI Designer & Art Director based in Paris.

Born in Beirut, I grew up between the United Arab Emirates and Lebanon before moving to Paris, where I enrolled in a graphic design graduate program at The Higher Institute of Applied Arts - LISAA.

After working in a creative studio for 5 years as an Art Director and Webdesigner, my growing interest in Human-Computer interactions and attention to users led me to pursue a career in UX/UI Design.

When I'm not solving problems, you can find me at a concert or on a plane, seeking new adventures.
